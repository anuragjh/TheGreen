Lucky Bamboo

Dracaena sanderiana is a species of flowering plant in the family Asparagaceae, native to Central Africa. It was named after the German–English gardener Henry Frederick Conrad Sander (1847–1920).

Spider Plant

Spider plant is a common name for several plants and may refer to: Chlorophytum comosum, the commonly cultivated houseplant Chlorophytum, a genus of plants in the asparagus family Saxifraga flagellaris, also known as whiplash saxifrage Cleome, a genus of flowering plants.

White Peace-Lily

Spathiphyllum is a genus of about 47 species of monocotyledonous flowering plants in the family Araceae, native to tropical regions of the Americas and southeastern Asia. Certain species of Spathiphyllum are commonly known as spath or peace lilies.
